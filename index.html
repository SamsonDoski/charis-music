The Journey

A structured path from beginner to pro

Every student follows a proven progression — theory, technique, and ear-training woven together so you grow with confidence and consistency.

Level 01

Beginner

⏱ ~6 months
  • Basic music theory, notes & rhythm
  • Instrument handling & posture
  • Finger exercises & coordination
  • Scales, timing & chord basics
  • Play common & favorite songs confidently
Level 02

Intermediate

⏱ ~12 months
  • Advanced theory & harmonic movement
  • Ear training & note recognition
  • Improvisation & playing by ear
  • Accompany songs you've never heard
  • Speed, accuracy & musical expression
Level 03

Advanced / Pro

⏱ Ongoing mastery
  • Modal theory & advanced harmony
  • Reharmonization & chord substitutions
  • Jazz applications across all genres
  • Relative pitch & advanced soloing
  • Full command of your instrument

🎓 Every level concludes with a Certificate of Completion & Instrument Proficiency Recognition.

Meet Your Teacher

Olajide Olanipekun

I started learning music at age 9, and through years of practice developed trained ears and relative pitch early — which let me understand and learn music faster. Today I play piano, all three saxophones, electric, bass, acoustic & semi-acoustic guitar, violin and recorder.

I served as Head Instrumentalist and Choir Director for the Teens & Youth arm of Living Spring Chapel International Church before relocating to the U.S., where I still serve as a consultant and advisor.

The program is backed by Charis Music School, founded by Dapo Olanipekun — which has trained 300+ students since 2019, including university professors, and authored multiple published music manuals.
